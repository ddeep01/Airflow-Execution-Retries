Experiment 1: Airflow Task Behavior

We executed a failing task to observe Airflow's internal behavior.

Observations:

1. Task starts in RUNNING state
2. On failure, it transitions to UP_FOR_RETRY
3. Airflow automatically retries the task
4. Retry count increases (try_number)
5. After maximum retries, task becomes FAILED

Logs confirm:

Retry attempt: 1
Retry attempt: 2
Retry attempt: 3
Retry attempt: 4

Conclusion:
Airflow handles task failures by automatically retrying based on configured parameters.
